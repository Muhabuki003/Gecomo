Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

GECOMO ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or place an order, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using this site you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Order information. When you check out, our commerce partner Shopify collects your name, billing and shipping address, email address, phone number, and payment details on our behalf. We never see or store your full payment card number.
  • Contact information. If you sign up for our email list, contact support, or submit a review, we collect your email address and anything you choose to include in your message.
  • Order tracking. If you use our order tracking page, the order number and email address you enter are passed to our tracking partner to look up your shipment.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data. Like most websites, our hosting and content-delivery providers automatically log basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, and referring URLs.
  • Local storage. We use your browser's local storage to remember the contents of your shopping cart between visits. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

How we use your information

  • To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping, returns, and our 60-day money-back guarantee.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and responses to your questions.
  • To send marketing emails you've signed up for — you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who need it to run our store:

  • Shopify — hosts our product catalog and processes checkout and payments. See the Shopify Privacy Policy.
  • Shipping carriers and our tracking partner (Track123) — to deliver your order and show you live tracking status.
  • Infrastructure providers (such as our web host, content-delivery network, and font provider Google Fonts) — which may process technical data like your IP address to serve the site.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

Cookies and similar technologies

Our own pages use browser local storage (not tracking cookies) to keep your cart. Shopify's checkout uses cookies that are necessary to complete your purchase. We do not run third-party advertising trackers on this site. You can clear local storage and cookies at any time in your browser settings; doing so will empty your saved cart.

Data retention

Order records are kept as long as needed to provide service, honor guarantees and returns, and meet tax and accounting requirements. Marketing email addresses are kept until you unsubscribe. You can ask us to delete your data at any time (see "Your rights" below).

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the GDPR for EEA/UK residents and the CCPA/CPRA for California residents), you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@gecomo.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law. California residents: we do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CPRA.

Children's privacy

Our products and website are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

Checkout is handled by Shopify over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections, and payment details are processed by PCI-DSS-compliant providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information.

International transfers

Our service providers may process your information in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision, and material changes will be posted on this page.

Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@gecomo.com.
